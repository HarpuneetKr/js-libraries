Top Attractions

The landmarks that define Toronto's skyline and spirit.

CN Tower

Soar 553 metres above the city for panoramic views from the observation deck or the thrilling Glass Floor.

Distillery District

Victorian industrial architecture transformed into a pedestrian-only village of galleries, boutiques, and acclaimed restaurants.

Toronto Islands

A short ferry ride from downtown, this car-free oasis offers beaches, parks, and unbeatable skyline views.

Royal Ontario Museum

World-class exhibits spanning natural history, world cultures, and art — all under Daniel Libeskind's iconic crystal.

Kensington Market

A bohemian neighbourhood packed with vintage shops, global street food, and the most vibrant street art in the city.

A lakeside neighbourhood with a boardwalk, sandy beach, and a relaxed small-town vibe.
